# 📱 Used Phone Khareedne Se Pehle — Mukammal Asaan Guide (Beginners Ke Liye)

Used phone khareedna pehli baar mein bohot mushkil lagta hai. Dil mein darr hota hai ke "kahin paise barbaad na ho jayein," "kahin phone block na ho jaye," ya "kahin chori ka phone na nikle." Bilkul ghabraane ki baat nahi! Yeh guide aap ko qadam-ba-qadam (step-by-step) sab kuch sikhayegi. Pura test sirf 20-25 minute ka hai aur is ke baad aap ek expert ki tarah phone check kar sakenge.

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Phone hamesha din ki roshni mein aur kisi public jagah (jaise market, mall, ya cafe) par check karo. Andhere ya akele jagah par mat jao.

---

## 🔧 Pehla Kaam: 3 Free Apps Install Karein

Phone dekhne jaane se pehle apne mobile mein yeh 3 apps Google Play Store se download kar lein. Internet kam lage to seller ke ghar/dukaan ka Wi-Fi mat istemaal karna — apna mobile data use karo (taake aap ke private results seller ko na nazar aayein).

### 📱 App #1: Phone Doctor Plus (Sab Cheezein Ek Saath Test Karta Hai)

**Yeh kya karta hai?** Yeh app phone ka "doctor" hai jo 40+ tests ek hi jagah par chala deta hai. Aap ko alag alag apps install karne ki zaroorat nahi.

📊 **Trust Info:** Google Play Store par 1 million+ se zyada downloads aur 4.1 star rating. Bilkul free hai.

**Yeh inn cheezon ko check karta hai:**

#### Screen aur Display:
- **Touch Screen Test** — Screen par koi "dead spot" to nahi (woh jagah jahan ungli rakhne par phone respond nahi karta)
- **Display Test** — Red, Green, Blue, White, Black — yeh sare colors screen par chala kar dikhata hai. Agar koi spot mein color theek na aaye, to "dead pixel" hai.
- **Multi-Touch Test** — Phone ek waqt mein 5-10 ungliyan pehchaan sakta hai ya nahi (gaming aur typing ke liye zaroori)

#### Awaaz aur Vibration:
- **Speaker Test** — Awaaz tezi se aati hai ya halki, kahin phati hui to nahi
- **Earpiece Test** — Call ke waqt jis se awaaz aati hai woh chhota speaker
- **Microphone Test** — Aap ki awaaz sahi record hoti hai ya nahi
- **Vibration Test** — Phone vibrate karta hai ya motor kharab hai

#### Sensors (Andar lage chhote chips jo phone ko smart banate hain):
- **Proximity Sensor** — Call ke waqt phone kaan se laga to screen automatic band hoti hai
- **Light Sensor** — Andhere mein screen automatic mand (dim) hoti hai
- **Gyroscope/Accelerometer** — Phone ghumane par screen ghumti hai (PUBG/games ke liye zaroori)
- **Magnetic Sensor (Compass)** — Direction sahi batata hai
- **GPS** — Google Maps mein sahi location pakarta hai

#### Connectivity:
- **Wi-Fi Test** — Internet connection theek hai
- **Bluetooth Test** — Wireless devices se connect hota hai
- **SIM Test** — SIM card ko sahi pehchanta hai

#### Hardware:
- **Charging Port Test** — Charger lagane wali jagah saaf-suthri aur kaam karne wali hai
- **Battery Health** — Battery kitni purani hai (lekin AccuBattery zyada accurate hai)
- **Camera Test** — Aage (selfie) aur peechay (back) dono camera, flashlight, autofocus
- **Fingerprint aur Face Unlock** — Lock kholne ke tareeqe theek kaam karte hain

**Istemaal kaise karein?**
1. App kholo
2. "Hardware Test" pe tap karo
3. Har test ek-ek karke chalao
4. App khud green tick (✅) ya red cross (❌) dikhayega
5. Agar koi test fail ho, to woh bara red flag hai — phone mat khareedo ya price kam karwao

### 🔋 App #2: Battery Guru (Battery Ki Asli Sehat Janne Ke Liye)

> ⚠️ **Important Update:** Pehle log "AccuBattery" use karte the, lekin ab Battery Guru zyada behtar aur reliable option hai. Yeh free hai aur same kaam karta hai (5M+ downloads, 4.5 stars).

**Yeh kya karta hai?** Yeh battery ki "asli sehat" batata hai — bilkul jaise doctor blood test se sehat batata hai.

**Samjhne wali baat:** Har battery par "mAh" likha hota hai (jaise 5000 mAh) — yeh battery ki naye waqt ki capacity hai. Lekin jaise jaise phone purana hota hai, yeh capacity kam hoti jati hai.

**Misal:** Naya phone — 5000 mAh full charge mein chalta tha 1 din. 2 saal baad — sirf 3500 mAh, isliye sirf 6-7 ghante chalta hai.

**Yeh app aap ko batayega:**

| Cheez | Iska Matlab |
|---|---|
| Asli Capacity | "5000 mAh likha hai, asal mein 4200 mAh hai" |
| Battery Health % | "85%" — matlab 15% kharab ho chuki hai |
| Charging Speed | Fast charging chal rahi hai ya slow |
| Discharge Rate | Battery normal speed se khatam ho rahi hai ya tezi se |
| Temperature | Charging ke waqt battery zyada garam to nahi ho rahi |

#### 🎯 Simple Khareedne Ke Rules:

- ✅ **90%+ Health** = Excellent, bilkul khareed lo
- ✅ **80-90% Health** = Acceptable, theek hai
- ⚠️ **70-80% Health** = Sochna paregi, price kam karwao
- ❌ **70% se kam** = Mat khareedo! Jaldi nayi battery dalwani paregi (4,000-15,000 PKR ka kharcha)

> **Note:** Sab se accurate result ke liye phone ko 1-2 baar full charge karna parta hai. Lekin foran bhi roughly andaza ho jata hai. Agar seller "battery foran test mat karo" kahe — yeh danger sign hai.

### 🔍 App #3: Device Info HW (Phone Asli Hai Ya Naqli — Confirm Karne Ke Liye)

> 💡 **Update:** Pehle "CPU-Z" use karte the — woh bhi achi app hai. Lekin Device Info HW specifically fake/clone phones pakarne ke liye banayi gayi hai aur Pakistan ke liye behtar hai.

**Pakistan mein yeh masla kyun zaroori hai?** Bohot saare "fake" ya "clone" Chinese phones bechay jate hain — bahar se Samsung S24 ya iPhone 15 lagte hain, lekin andar se duplicate hote hain. Aap 80,000 ka phone samajh kar khareedte ho, asal mein 15,000 ka chinese clone hota hai.

**Misal:** Seller bole "Yeh Samsung Galaxy S24 hai, 12GB RAM, 256GB storage, Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor." Aap Device Info HW chala kar foran dekh sakte ho — sach mein yeh sare specs hain, ya jhoot bol raha hai.

**Yeh app aap ko dikhayega:**
- Asli Processor ka naam (Snapdragon, MediaTek, Apple chip, etc.)
- Asli RAM kitni hai (kabhi 8GB likha hota hai, asal mein 4GB hota hai)
- Asli Storage capacity
- Asli Android Version — fake phones aksar Android version ke baare mein bhi jhoot bolte hain
- Phone ka model number aur serial number
- Sare sensors ki list (asli phones mein sare sensors hote hain, fake mein kuch missing hote hain)

✨ **Khaas Feature:** Device Info HW agar fake specs detect kare to khud "FAKE" likh ke warning deta hai!

**Confirm karne ka asaan tareeqa:**
1. App kholo
2. Sare details note karo (RAM, processor, storage, model)
3. Doosre phone ya browser mein [GSMArena.com](https://www.gsmarena.com) kholo
4. Phone ka model search karo (jaise "Samsung Galaxy S24")
5. Official specs se compare karo
6. Agar ek bhi cheez match na ho — phone fake hai, bilkul mat khareedo!

## 🇵🇰 PTA Approval Check Karna (Pakistan Mein Sab Se Zaroori Step)

### PTA Hai Kya?

PTA matlab **Pakistan Telecommunication Authority** — yeh government ka department hai jo Pakistan mein mobile networks aur phones ko control karta hai. Har phone ko PTA ke paas register hona zaroori hai, warna woh phone Pakistan mein nahi chal sakta.

### Yeh Itna Important Kyun Hai?

Agar phone PTA approved nahi hai (yani "non-PTA"), to:
- Naya phone hai to 60 din chalega, phir block ho jayega
- Block hone ke baad koi SIM nahi chalegi — na call, na SMS, na mobile data
- Phone bas Wi-Fi pe chalega — yani ghar ke andar tak mehdood (limited)
- Resale value bilkul khatam ho jati hai
- Tax bhar ke approve karwana parta hai jo bohot mehnga hai

---

### Step 1: Pehle IMEI Number Nikalo

#### IMEI Hai Kya?

IMEI har phone ka unique ID number hota hai — bilkul jaise har insaan ka apna CNIC alag hota hai. Yeh 15 digits ka hota hai. Duniya ke har phone ka IMEI alag hota hai.

#### Nikalne Ka Tareeqa:

Phone ke dialer (jahan call ke liye number type karte hain) mein yeh code likhiye:

```
*#06#
```

Bas itna likhte hi screen par IMEI khud-ba-khud aa jayega — call button mat dabana.

> ⚠️ **Dual-SIM Phones:** Aaj kal zyada phones mein 2 SIM lagne ki jagah hoti hai. Aise phones mein 2 IMEI numbers aate hain. DONO note karo — yeh bohot zaroori hai (neechay tip #1 mein wajah samjhayi gayi hai).

**IMEI doosri jagah jahan mil sakta hai:**
- Phone ke box ke peechay sticker par
- Settings → About Phone → Status mein
- SIM tray (jahan SIM lagti hai) ke andar likha hua
- iPhone walay: Settings → General → About

---

### Step 2: PTA Status Check Karne Ke 4 Tareeqe

#### ⭐ Tareeqa #1: SMS Through 8484 (Sab Se Asaan — Beginners Ke Liye Best)

1. Phone mein SMS app kholo
2. New message likho
3. Sirf 15 digit ka IMEI number likho (aur kuch mat likho — koi space, dash, ya word nahi)
4. Number ki jagah **8484** likho
5. Send dabao

Thodi der mein reply aayega. Jawab inn 4 mein se ek hoga:

| Reply | Iska Matlab | Faisla |
|---|---|---|
| ✅ "Compliant" ya "PTA Approved" | Phone safe hai, hamesha chalega | Khareed sakte ho ✅ |
| ⚠️ "Valid (60 days)" ya "Valid till [date]" | Naya phone, 60 din mein tax dena hoga | Soch ke khareedo — tax ka kharcha jodo |
| ❌ "Non-Compliant" | Tax nahi diya, jaldi block ho jayega | Mat khareedo ❌ |
| 🔒 "Blocked" | Phone pehle se dead hai | Bilkul mat khareedo! ❌❌ |

Yeh free hai aur har network par chalti hai (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, Ufone).

> ⚠️ **Dual-SIM Note:** Dono IMEI alag-alag SMS kar ke check karo. Dono ka jawab "Compliant" aana chahiye.

#### Tareeqa #2: PTA DVS App (Mobile Pe App Se Check)

> 📌 **Naam ki Tabdeeli:** Pehle yeh "DIRBS" app kehlati thi, ab official naam "DVS" (Device Verification System) hai. PTA ki khud ki banayi hui app hai.

1. Google Play Store kholo
2. "DVS PTA" search karo (developer: pk.gov.dirbs)
3. Download karo
4. App mein IMEI haath se daalo, ya phone ke box ka barcode scan karo

#### Tareeqa #3: PTA DIRBS Website

Browser mein jao: [https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk](https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk)

IMEI number daalo aur "Check" pe click karo. Same result milega.

#### Tareeqa #4: PTA Ki Main Website

[https://www.pta.gov.pk](https://www.pta.gov.pk) par jao → "Device Verification System" pe click → IMEI daalo

> ✅ **Best Practice:** SMS aur Website dono se check karo. Kabhi kabhi database update mein der ho jati hai.

## ⚠️ 7 Bohot Zaroori Tips — Yeh Galtiyan Mat Karna!

**1. Dual-SIM phone ke DONO IMEI alag-alag check karo**
Yeh sab se common scam hai. Aksar ek IMEI approved hota hai aur doosra "Non-Compliant" — sellers sirf approved wala dikhate hain. Dono ka result "Compliant" aana lazmi hai, warna phone ka ek SIM slot useless ho jayega.

**2. Seller ka screenshot bilkul mat maano**
Sellers purani, photoshopped, ya kisi aur phone ki screenshots dikhate hain. Apne saamne, apne phone mein IMEI dial karwao aur khud SMS bhejo. Apne haath se test karo, apni aankhon se dekho.

**3. Box, phone, aur receipt — teeno par IMEI same hona chahiye**
Phone se *#06# se nikla IMEI, box ke peechay likha IMEI, aur receipt par likha IMEI — teeno bilkul same hone chahiye. Agar farq ho — box badla gaya hai ya phone chori ka ho sakta hai. Bhag jao.

**4. "Sirf Wi-Fi pe chalega, PTA ki zaroorat nahi" — yeh dhoka hai**
Bina PTA ke phone mein 60 din baad koi SIM nahi chalegi. Internet sirf Wi-Fi pe milega — ghar se bahar phone bekaar ho jayega. Inn baaton mein bilkul mat aana.

**5. Phone "Charge" hua hua chahiye test ke waqt**
Seller se pehle hi keh do "Phone full charge ho ke aana." Agar woh "battery low" ka bahana banaye, to samjho kuch chupana hai (shayad battery jaldi khatam ho jati hai).

**6. Find My iPhone / Google Account zaroor sign-out karwao**
- iPhone: Settings → [Naam] → Sign Out (Apple ID password se)
- Android: Settings → Accounts → Google → Remove Account

Aap ke saamne karwao, baad mein nahi! Agar pichla account na hate, to phone bricked ho jata hai (useless).

**7. Original receipt aur warranty card lo**
Original receipt aap ko warranty, future resale, aur kisi masle (jaise chori ki investigation) mein bachayegi. Agar seller na de — soch ke khareedo.

## 💰 PTA Tax — Kitna Kharcha Aata Hai? (2026 Updated Rates)

> 📰 **Latest Update:** January 2026 mein FBR ne used mobile phones par PTA tax kam kar diya hai (Valuation Ruling No. 2076/2026). Lekin tax abhi bhi kafi hai.

Agar phone non-PTA hai aur aap khud approve karwana chahte hain, to FBR ko tax dena parta hai. Tax phone ki price aur model ke hisaab se badalta hai:

| Phone Category | Tax Range (Approx.) |
|---|---|
| Sasta phone (Tecno, Infinix, Xiaomi entry-level) | 15,000 - 25,000 PKR |
| Mid-range phone (Samsung A-series, Redmi Note) | 25,000 - 45,000 PKR |
| iPhone 13/14/15 | 80,000 - 130,000+ PKR |
| iPhone 15 Pro Max | ~50,604 PKR (CNIC) / ~44,984 PKR (Passport) |
| Latest Flagships (Samsung S24, iPhone 16) | 100,000 - 160,000+ PKR |

> 💡 **Passport vs CNIC:** Passport par tax thoda kam hota hai (lekin sirf overseas Pakistanis use kar sakte hain, sirf 60 din mein, aur sirf ek phone). CNIC par tax zyada hota hai but local log yahi use karte hain.

**Latest sahi tax calculate karne ke liye:**
- Official: [https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk](https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk) (PTA Tax Calculator)
- Asaan: [https://ptataxcalculator.com](https://ptataxcalculator.com)

## 🧮 Asaan Hisaab Lagao — Faida Ya Nuksaan?

Maan lo seller bole: "Yeh non-PTA iPhone 13 sirf 1,20,000 ka — 30,000 sasta!"

**Calculate karo:**
- Non-PTA price: 1,20,000 PKR
- PTA tax (CNIC): ~85,000 PKR
- **Total: 2,05,000 PKR**
- Lekin PTA approved iPhone 13 used market mein: ~1,80,000 PKR

**Result:** Non-PTA phone khareedna 25,000 PKR ka nuksaan hai!

**Sabaq:** Aksar non-PTA phones tax ke baad mehngay parte hain. Sirf wahi non-PTA khareedo agar (a) aap calculation karke confirm karo ke sasta hai, ya (b) overseas se aa raha hai aur passport scheme use karo.

- [ ] Dual-SIM hai to dono IMEI check kiye
- [ ] Box ka IMEI, phone ka IMEI, receipt ka IMEI — teeno match

### Apps Se Test
- [ ] Phone Doctor Plus se sare hardware tests pass ho gaye
- [ ] Battery Guru se battery health 80%+ confirm hui

- [ ] Apni SIM dali, kisi ko call ki — dono taraf awaaz saaf

## 🚩 Yeh Red Flags Dekhe — Bhag Jao!

Agar inn mein se koi bhi cheez ho, to phone bilkul mat khareedo:

- 🚩 Seller jaldi machaaye ya keh "abhi paise do warna doosra grahak le jayega"
- 🚩 Phone test karne na de ya specific tests rok de
- 🚩 IMEI match na ho box/phone/receipt ke beech
- 🚩 Sirf charger ke saath chalu ho (battery dead)
- 🚩 Phone bohot garam ho jaye normal use mein
- 🚩 Original receipt na ho aur seller ki kahani badalti rahe
- 🚩 Account sign-out karne mein der lagaye ya bahana banaye
- 🚩 "Wi-Fi pe chalega bas" — yeh poori tarah dhoka hai
- 🚩 Cheap price jo market rate se bohot kam ho (chori ka phone hone ka chance)
