## Tips for Choosing Your E-Commerce Palette

1. **Limit to 3–5 colors** — Primary, Secondary, Accent, Background, and Text.
2. **Ensure contrast ratios** meet WCAG accessibility standards (4.5:1 for text).
3. **Use your CTA color sparingly** so buttons stand out and drive clicks.
4. **Test on real screens** — colors render differently on mobile vs. desktop.
5. **Match your brand personality** — the palette should feel like your product.
6. **Avoid trendy-only palettes** — timeless colors age better.
