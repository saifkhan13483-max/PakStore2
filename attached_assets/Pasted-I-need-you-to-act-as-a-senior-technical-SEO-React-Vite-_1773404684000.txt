My diagnosis of your likely root causes
Based on your screenshots + live checks, the most likely issues are:

1) Google is not seeing enough strong HTML on dynamic pages
Because PakCart is an SPA, product/category pages may initially look weak before JS finishes.

That matters a lot for:

/products/:slug
/collections/:slug
If these pages are discoverable in sitemap but Google gets weak render confidence, they can sit in Discovered – currently not indexed.

2) Internal discovery is probably weaker than you think
Google indexes pages faster when they are linked from:

homepage
category pages
related products
breadcrumbs
navigation
If product pages are mostly reachable through client-side interactions, filters, sliders, or stateful UI, that hurts indexing.

3) Sitemap may be incomplete or not reliably returning all dynamic URLs
Your technical description says api/sitemap.ts should include products/categories, but the live fetched sitemap output I got showed only a small static set. That’s a major thing to investigate.

Possible reasons:

Firestore admin credentials not loading correctly in the Vercel function
fallback path returning only static URLs
dynamic sections failing silently
malformed XML or fetch timeout causing partial output
wrong deployment target/version
This is one of the first things I’d fix.

4) Collection pages may be especially weak
I found no obvious indexed /collections/... URLs from search. That suggests:

they may not be linked strongly enough,
they may not be in sitemap properly,
or they may render too thinly / too late.
